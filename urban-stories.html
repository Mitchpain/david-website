Urban Stories

2023 · narrative · 22 min

Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing city, Urban Stories weaves together three interconnected narratives exploring themes of displacement, belonging, and urban transformation. Through the lives of a street photographer, a construction worker, and a late-night taxi driver, the film captures the pulse of contemporary urban life and the human stories often overlooked in the rush of modern development.
